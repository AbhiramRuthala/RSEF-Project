Alzheimer's Disease (AD) Speech-Tracking Technology

Welcome! You can test your susceptibility of developing Alzheimer's Disease through this voice-recording system, that will track components such as speech deficiency, sentence formation, pauses etc. Press the button to start.

Essentially, by pressing the button, a recording of your speech will start. In this recoding, say whatever you want. We've left a list of questions for you to answer below to make this easier.

Once you complete the recording, a report will be downloaded onto your computer demonstrating the susceptibility of you developing Alzheimer's Disease and any stats that show so.


List of questions:

  • Tell me about yourself?
  • What do you do in a regular day?
  • What is the best hot take that you have?
  • How would you describe yourself?
  • What is your funniest story?

Once ready, press the button and start recording.
